Dr. Sheldon Cooper

Dr. Sheldon Cooper is a theortecial physicist who works at Caltech. He and his wife, neurobiologist Dr. Amy Farrah Fowler, won the 2019 Nobel Prize in Physics for their work on super-symmetry.

Dr. Leonard Hofstadter

Dr. Leonard Hofstadter is an experimental physicist at Caltech. Hofstater and Cooper wrote a paper detailing a theory that space-time is like a super-fluid. It was well received by the scientific community.
